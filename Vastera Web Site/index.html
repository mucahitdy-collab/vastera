THE VISION

Reconstructing Deep Time with Cinematic Precision.

VASTERA is an independent documentary project dedicated to uncovering the forgotten eons of our planet. From molten surfaces under relentless bombardment to ice-locked oceans and vast prehistoric supercontinents, we bridge cutting-edge scientific research with high-end visual storytelling.

Designed for thinkers, explorers, and lovers of natural history, every episode offers an immersive journey into the forces that sculpted our existence.

CHRONOLOGY

4.54 Billion Years of Planetary History

Journey through Earth's geological eons and eras.

4.54 Billion Years Ago

Hadean Eon

The violent birth of Earth. A realm of molten rock, asteroid impacts, and the colossal collision that formed our Moon.

4.00 Billion Years Ago

Archean Eon

Cooling crust and liquid oceans appear. The first microscopic life emerges around deep-sea hydrothermal vents.

2.50 Billion Years Ago

Proterozoic Eon

The Great Oxidation Event transforms the atmosphere, triggering global "Snowball Earth" glaciations and complex cells.

538 Million Years Ago

Cambrian Explosion

An unprecedented evolutionary burst creating hard shells, compound eyes, and predators like Anomalocaris.

298 Million Years Ago

Permian Period

Supercontinent Pangaea forms. Ends in the "Great Dying"—the most severe extinction event in Earth's history (96% species lost).

201 Million Years Ago

Jurassic Period

Lush, warm greenhouse climate dominated by giant sauropods, stegosaurs, and marine reptiles in shallow seas.

145 Million Years Ago

Cretaceous Period

First flowering plants emerge alongside T-Rex and Triceratops, ending with the Chicxulub asteroid impact 66 Mya.

66 Million Years Ago

Cenozoic Era

The Age of Mammals. Continents drift to modern positions, ice ages reshape landscapes, and humanity rises.
